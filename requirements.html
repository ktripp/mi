The MI Consultants team is skilled at generating in-depth descriptions of new products and services. We have successfully translated business plans into detailed product descriptions and implementable product and engineering requirements.

We collaborate with the client team to create product or service descriptions that are derived from use-cases. We then use this documentation to drive product requirements, engineering requirements, and test requirements while maintaining traceability throughout the process. These steps form the link between strategic product/service plans and successful implementation of the solution.

Requirements Generation and Traceability

Where we can help: Your Challenges
  • Products or services are not fully defined or documented.
  • Product plans are not driven by use-cases based upon end-user needs.
  • The development team gets sidetracked or needs constant attention in meetings to understand the product functionality to be implemented.
  • Requirements are not effectively managed.
  • Formal change control process is deficient.
  • Documentation does not exist to leverage organizational assets and use lessons-learned from previous projects.
What you can receive from MI Consultants: Our Deliverables
  • Clearly defined use-cases or user-story documentation to describe functionality and enhance requirements generation.
  • Documented and approved product or project requirements developed from business strategy, market research, and project charter.
  • Technical requirements to support development.
  • Requirements traceability matrix linking user-stories, development plans, and acceptance criteria.
  • Comprehensive documentation and building lessons learned.

RFI/RFP Generation and Response Analysis

Where we can help: Your Challenges
  • Process to manage the RFI / RFP cycle is time-consuming and complicated.
  • Requirements to support an RFI / RFP are non-existent or lack adequate detail for vendors to respond.
  • Requirements have multi-level questions that allow for easy vendor acceptance.
  • Excessive vendor communications.
  • Requirements that do not support vendor selection objectives.
  • No formal process exists to identify, qualify, and select vendors.
What you can receive from MI Consultants: Our Deliverables
  • End-to-end RFI / RFP process execution that includes vendor engagement, communications, response formats, templates, rating system, and selection criteria.
  • Analysis of the vendor responses and vendor selection recommendation.
  • Business case and product or service impact analysis.
