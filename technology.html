Mobile Impact's depth of technical knowledge and engineering skill allows us to support any part of the wireless ecosystem including product development, lab configurations, application deployment, and network/IT systems. Our team has the breadth of knowledge and skill to assist clients in identifying applicable new technologies, network standards, device operating systems, and applications, thus determining how those are best integrated into products, services, and support tools.

  • Specifications: The Mobile Impact team can provide the technical foundation for most solutions. Our rich background in engineering development provides the fundamental understanding of technology to effectively create engineering specifications.
  • Engineering Solutions: The Mobile Impact team has designed and developed many hardware and software mobile solutions, and can successfully assist our clients in core technology choices. We can also identify, recommend, broker, and manage 3rd party engineering companies to design new products and services.
  • Wireless Device Certification: In addition to performing test engineering services, we analyze test results and continually review how the client can improve the throughput, quality, and performance of their certification process. Our recommendations are based on a solid background from developing wireless products and a hands-on knowledge of working on device certification. We can generate device certification strategies, test processes, plans, and scripts for new products and services or we can design a test system from the ground up.
  • Technology Standards: As a team, Mobile Impact's years of wireless engineering development is invaluable when analyzing technical Standards to advise our clients on the deployment or development of that Standard within their products or services. We are able to articulate the important features of the Standard and interpret the impact of the Standards. We also represent our client's interests in tracking and proposing changes to Standards.
