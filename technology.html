MI Consultants' depth of technical knowledge and engineering skill allows us to support any part of the product or system development process. Our forte is systems engineering and technology development. Our team is well versed in translating product requirements into system diagrams, engineering specifications, technical requirements, and test suites. We assist clients in identifying applicable new technologies, creating specifications, developing system architectures, enhancing mobility systems, driving wireless access technologies, and identifying compatible equipment. Our team is adept at utilizing applications, support tools, and completing systems integration.

Engineering Solutions

Where we can help: Your Challenges
  • Development teams are overtasked dealing with existing products and services.
  • Product requirements and specifications lack depth to guide development activities.
  • Test suites are poorly conceived and produce inconsistent results.
  • Internal engineering skills are unavailable to execute new product initiatives.
What you can receive from MI Consultants: Our Deliverables
  • Engineering specifications and technical requirements for new products or services.
  • Analysis to identify and recommend core technology selection.
  • Identification, recommendation, brokering, and management of 3rd party engineering companies to design new products and services.
  • Test suites derived from requirements.
  • Validated solutions.

Product or Service Validation

Where we can help: Your Challenges
  • Product test or service evaluation is late or not run to completion.
  • Test processes, certification process, or test plans are not documented or not understood by stakeholders.
  • Product test and certification objectives are not defined.
  • Products released to market with known issues or problems.
  • Requirements not updated to reflect product changes.
  • Failures reported from field after release.
What you can receive from MI Consultants: Our Deliverables
  • Documentation that outlines the end-to-end test and certification process.
  • Product acceptance criteria for launch.
  • Test fault coverage tied to product requirements.
  • Documented test system definition and test suites.
  • Lab and field test execution and documentation.

Technology Standards

  • Development teams are not positioned to evaluate potential new technologies.
  • Feature identification lacks technical feasibility and analysis.
  • New standards are unknown, difficult to understand, or do not match product roadmaps.
What you can receive from MI Consultants: Our Deliverables
  • Documentation that interprets the impact of new technology to your systems and roadmaps.
