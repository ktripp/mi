Our knowledge of business operations is enhanced by our background in IT systems and technology and allows us to provide invaluable insight to help our clients improve their operational performance. We quickly analyze processes and systems to outline potential performance improvements and provide directions to help navigate and implement changes.

We assist supply chain organizations in collecting meaningful information from across the organization and vendors, which allows our clients to make optimal purchase decisions. We can manage the supply chain and create processes to execute effectively.

Process Development and Process Re-design

Where we can help: Your Challenges
  • Processes are undocumented or ad hoc.
  • Taking product or service from concept to launch is fraught with trouble, scope creep, and time lost.
  • Product validation varies and testing is repeated or not completed.
  • Product teams, development teams, and test teams are not communicating.
  • Users resist new technology and systems due to lack of familiarity or difficulty in initial experience with the solution.
What you can receive from MI Consultants: Our Deliverables
  • Neutral perspective to create new processes or redefine existing processes.
  • Documentation and mapping of “As-Is” processes.
  • Definition, documentation, and implementation of “To-Be” processes.
  • Deployment, training, transition, and management on new processes.
  • Comprehensive documentation to baseline organizational process assets.

Supply Chain Support

Where we can help: Your Challenges
  • Comparison information or benchmarking data is incomplete or not organized.
  • Total cost of ownership is not understood.
  • Contracts lack requirements or appropriate metrics for monitoring product/service purchases and vendor performance.
  • Supply chain team requires technical depth to support purchase of specialized equipment.
  • Outsourcing analysis is biased by the attitudes of the organization.
What you can receive from MI Consultants: Our Deliverables
  • Well documented product analysis and benchmarking comparisons based upon in-depth research.
  • Collection of meaningful information from across the organization and vendors, allowing informed purchase decisions.
  • Processes to effectively manage the supply chain and negotiate contracts.
  • Objective root-cause analysis and corrective action planning.
  • Total cost of ownership models and metrics.
  • Objective recommendation on outsourcing make-vs-buy decisions.
