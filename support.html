Our knowledge of the wireless business and carrier operations provides invaluable insight to help our clients improve their operational performance. The current economic environment requires optimizing productivity and thus lowering operational costs. We can quickly analyze the impacts of changes and provide directions to help navigate through the changes.

  • Process Development and Process Re-design: We help our clients develop new processes to support new products/services or re-design processes to support new initiatives. We work closely with the subject matter experts to identify and document “As-Is” processes and then develop the new “To-Be” processes, utilizing a neutral perspective to identify the most suitable solution. Following completion of the “To-Be” process, we can then lead the deployment of the new process and assist our clients in transitioning Executive Team through the impact of those changes.
  • Business Process Outsourcing: We assist clients in evaluating business processes and the resulting effects to the organization for outsourcing operational functions. Our broad experience and unique insights can be leveraged to provide strategic analysis and tactical support during the entire decision making process.
  • Vendor Contract Development: We work very closely with cross-functional subject matter experts of the client to document requirements in drafting contracts, amendments, service level agreements, etc. We lead or support the negotiation process while tracking changes and action items during negotiation. We provide clearly written final results/status and develop negotiating positions for our clients.
  • Logistics and Operations Support: We have an astute understanding of mobile product process flow, from forecasting to purchasing, inventory management, sales, and product returns. We help clients improve operational efficiency in logistics, and deliver new objectives throughout the operations team.
  • Supply Chain Support: Beyond vendor contracts, we assist supply chain organizations in collecting meaningful information from across the organization and vendors, which allows our clients to make informed purchase decisions. We help create processes to maintain and use such information to effectively manage the supply chain, including creating total cost of ownership models, establishing templates for vendor terms and conditions, and creating a baseline of product costs.
