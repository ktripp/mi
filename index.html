Experienced counsel, practical solutions, and professional execution

The Mobile Impact Group (MI Consultants) is a consultancy providing answers to address technology challenges and improve business operations of our clients. Our practitioners have a unique blend of skills and knowledge that we leverage into high quality, comprehensive solutions. We are equipped to quickly understand your obstacles and provide objective insight to rapidly define deliverables and execute projects.

We provide adept guidance on technology solutions that include IT, broadband, wireless systems, and equipment. Our services reach across technologies that include cellular, satellite, broadband, and WiFi, as well as, supporting market segments in education technology, telematics, enterprise IT/mobility, M2M, and mobile payments. We are committed to your success and we will work with you to meet your unique objectives.

MI Consultants provide solutions that can be applied across many organizations, technologies, or systems. We offer experienced counsel, practical solutions, and professional execution in:

  • Strategy, research, and analysis
  • Requirements driven product definition
  • Technology evaluation, engineering design, and product validation
  • Program planning and project execution
  • Operations support, financial analysis, and risk mitigation
