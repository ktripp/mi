Good business decisions and strategies start with a firm understanding of the market opportunity and technology options. Mobile Impact provides custom research of market conditions and technology solutions. Our extensive business experience and deep technical knowledge then allows us to "quick-start" projects and adeptly analyze the research data, yielding actionable strategies and forward recommendations for our clients.

  • Custom Market Research:Research on emerging new products, services, features, and technologies that may impact the client's product portfolio, product/service/feature roadmap, and technical solutions. Mobile Impact interviews and studies markets, vendors, competitors, and partners to unlock the not-so-obvious drivers and issues in the market. Our services combine RFI/RFP cycles, interviews, web research, and our internal knowledge as input to our analysis.
  • Device Portfolio Analysis: We provide an objective in depth analysis of the products, services, and features offered currently or planned to guide product positioning and differentiation in the portfolio and its associated ecosystems relative to the competition.
  • Business case generation and analysis: Mobile impact is well versed in developing cost benefit analysis, product/services profitability, return on investment analysis, and business case generation of new products, services, features, and technologies.
  • Cost Analysis and Benchmarking: Analysis of product manufacturing costs, and gross margins to allow comparisons across product types, features, technologies, wireless standards (e.g., CDMA vs. WCDMA), etc.
  • RFI/RFP Generation and Response Analysis: Defining, producing, and conducting RFI/RFP documentation successfully is a very involved process. Mobile Impact has a proven record of working with our clients to collect and generate effective requirements for the RFI or RFP, identify leading vendors for the desired solutions, analyzing the vendor responses, and making vendor selection recommendations.
