Broad experience in technology design and development, program execution, and business operations

MI Consultants solve problems and fosters strong partnerships with our clients. Our enduring rapport enables us to collaboratively determine optimal approaches and rapidly deliver successful solutions. We consider the whole solution, in the context of your organization and your industry, and provide objective insight regardless of how it may affect our involvement. Imagine, working with consultants that are focused on practical, real-world solutions to your needs and having a relationship built on trust and candor. Imagine, working with a consulting firm that flexibly adjusts scope in real-time, as necessary, to accommodate changing conditions. Imagine having a partner fully invested in your success!

MI Consultants was founded in 2007 by Shawn Bayle after recognizing that the consulting industry had slid into more of a one-off contractor methodology, limiting the synergies of teamwork. Shawn’s vision was to create a company that was focused on collaboration, both internally and with the client. To facilitate this, Shawn engaged a team of accomplished technologists and business executives to tap a wide base of skills, capable of providing meaningful and appropriate depth to every project. By adroitly combining these diverse skills and backgrounds, MI Consultants operate collectively to deliver projects efficiently and with higher quality.

We combine a rich engineering background, operational expertise, and sound business acumen to deliver each of our solutions. Our broad and deep experience has supported clients in virtually all functional groups of an organization – strategy, product development, engineering, finance, supply chain management, product management, marketing, and operations. Our client base includes many Fortune 100 companies as well as numerous startups and mid-market public and private organizations.
