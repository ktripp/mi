Broad experience in all aspects of the wireless ecosystem.

From its founding in 2007 by a group of savvy wireless industry veterans with extensive experience in business strategy, product management, operations, and engineering, Mobile Impact has provided business and technical consulting services to the wireless market place. We have broad experience in all aspects of the wireless ecosystem and we are especially adept in the areas of mobile devices, mobile data services, network systems, carrier operations, and supply chain management.

We combine a rich engineering background, wireless carrier operational expertise, and sound business acumen, to support each one of our diverse solutions in our project portfolio. Our broad and deep experience has supported clients in virtually all functional groups of an organization - strategy, product development, engineering, finance, supply chain management, product management, marketing, and operations.

We serve Fortune 100 clients and high-growth middle market firms primarily in telecommunications, consumer products, utilities, and the public sector.
