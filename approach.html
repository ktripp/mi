Client-focused:

MI Consultants will provide the optimum solution by carefully weighing your business objectives, your internal processes, and how your organization works in the context of your industry segment. We collaborate with you to formulate a plan that meets your objectives while minimizing project overhead, both in the development of project scope/terms and in the management of project execution. We provide streamlined recommendations that are customized to your specific needs. Our recommended process creates use-cases that drive requirements, establishes a solid execution plan, and forms a baseline to validate the solution.

Our experience allows us to rapidly understand project objectives and craft detailed proposals including:

  • Project background
  • Project goals and desired outcome
  • Work product and/or process
  • Deliverables
  • Resources
  • Time-lines
  • Terms and conditions

MI Consultants business models are flexible and are individually tailored to your needs. Our ability to adjust project deliverables enables us to quickly adapt the project to changing market needs and project conditions through real-time collaboration.


Teamwork:

Teamwork is a prominent aspect of our company delivered via a unique and powerful collaborative solutions model. For all of our projects, we leverage the collective skills and knowledge of our team to support individual consultants and we share that knowledge with our clients to help them formulate strategic solutions with broad outside perspective. Our consultants are able to synthesize internal knowledge with client data to deliver greater value, breadth, and quality than any one-off contractor or traditional staffing models.
