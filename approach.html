Client-focused:

Starting with a basic definition of the client's need or problem, Mobile Impact develops a proposal for addressing the challenge. Following client approval of the project outline, Mobile Impact works to define business terms through a flexible set of business models individually tailored to our clients and develops a detailed statement of work of the project that includes:

  • Project background
  • Project goals and desired outcome
  • Work product and/or process
  • Deliverables
  • Resources
  • Time-lines
  • Terms and conditions

With a firm description of the project, our team is able to quickly ramp up and immediately start providing value to the client. Our team then works with the client to analyze, document, plan, and deliver to project goals.


Teamwork:

Teamwork is a vital aspect of our company. For all of our projects, we not only leverage the collective skills and knowledge of our team within Mobile Impact to support our consultants, but we also share that knowledge to our clients to help them formulate strategic direction and provide outside perspective. The end result is that our consultants are not alone on a project and we are able to deliver greater value, breadth, quality, and flexibility than one-off contractors or traditional staffing models.
