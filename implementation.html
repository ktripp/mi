Mobile Impact has a track record of implementing programs and projects within budget and on time. We can be engaged at any stage and assist the client wherever needed. We review project documentation and make recommendations, formulate schedules, and manage resources to implement projects and launch any product.

  • Program and Project Management: Whether program managing the entire solution across an organization or tactically managing a specific portion of the project, Mobile Impact is adept at managing the dependencies between projects, including planning, organizing, securing, and managing resources to bring about the successful completion of specific project goals and objectives.
  • Client-to Server Integration: Modern mobile data systems require solutions that are implemented on both the device and network/server. Our rich history in the mobile data space gives Mobile Impact the ability to specify, deploy, and maintain fully integrated system solutions spanning one or more wireless networks and a wide array of client devices. We provide support for the development of partnerships and application ecosystems across OEMs, content providers, and multiple network providers, as well as, assist the client in integrating those solutions.
  • Product Launches: Our breadth of skills and experience allow us to support new product or service introductions. We can manage entire product launches or assist in introducing new features, releases, or functions to existing products.
