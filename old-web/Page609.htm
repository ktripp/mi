Value Proposition

Handset, Network Element, & Service Requirement Generation and Validation

Generation of Product or Service Requirements is perhaps the most important step in new product/service launches. Such requirements form the link between strategy and successful implementation.

Mobile Impact staff have been developing such Product Requirement Documents for over 20 years. We validate our requirements against the desired strategic plan and with the enabling technologies and vendors necessary to assure implementation in the desired time frame.

· Generating initial service requirements from strategic documentation

· Generating handset, network, and system specifications

· Updating existing requirements to new revision states or new service levels

· Migrating product requirements from one vendor to another

· Future proofing requirements

· Ensuring Consistency in Requirements
