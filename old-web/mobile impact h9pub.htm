Value Proposition

Mobile Impact Consulting Practice

The wireless industry is undergoing dramatic change as mobile data grows in importance to carriers and vendors. The advent of open mobile web services, compelling new applications, and smartphones are exponentially increasing the rate of use of mobile web services by consumers. Mobile Impact has built its practice around enabling operators and core technology companies to capitalize on these developments.

Mobile Impact provides professional and technical consulting services to the wireless industry in the areas of mobile data systems and handset product management. We view the mobile space as a client-server system and bring combined expertise in both mobile phones and network systems to our projects.

Our core competency is the development of product and service requirements using our highly technical background, supported by the development of sound business cases. As inputs to these requirements we provide strategy development/evaluation and custom research services. We support the implementation of these requirements with project and vendor management services.

Our team of Principal Consultants average 20 years of experience across all aspects of the industry. Whether our clients need requirements defined, implementations managed, or business goals aligned with technical enablers, our team is ready to provide high impact, real-world results with a dedication to quality and timeliness necessary in todays fast paced wireless world.

Our systems approach to new product and service launch combines network, handset, and software to assure project success.
