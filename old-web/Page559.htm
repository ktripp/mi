Value Proposition

Product and Service Deployment Strategies

Mobile Impact works with our Clients to develop industry leading, custom business strategies. Our approach uses decades of industry experience as a foundation and builds the strategy through a combination of research, analysis, knowledge of technical enablers, and detailed review of our Clients desired market position and current capabilities.

Our reports detail the proposed strategy, its costs and risks, and its projected business results. We enhance our reports with a thorough analysis of the supply chain and technical enablers needed to implement the proposed strategy.

Whether we develop a strategic recommendation or evaluate one already under consideration, Mobile Impact will provide the depth necessary to drive a successful launch.

· Custom product or service strategic development or review

· Business case development including benefit, cost, and risk

· Project and business scorecard development

· Recommendations developed including risks, rewards, and needed enablers

· Reports generated with an intent to implement vs. pure analytical approaches

· Rapid execution, detailed results
