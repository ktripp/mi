Value Proposition

Implementation Consulting Services

Moving to implementation from planning starts with the development of a project plan. Mobile Impact provides start to finish management services applying PMI techniques to monitor and report on project progress.

Our service goes beyond identifying project risks up front to the real-time contingency planning necessary to address changing business climates, development difficulties, and supply chain failures.

Using real-time tracking tools, our project managers provide regular, concise project reports to our clients along with proposals to address problems and issues in the project.

Mobile Impact also provides objective management coordination between vendors. Our focus is purely on achieving project milestones on time and at desired quality so we maintain complete objectivity relative to vendors and work with all equally to assure success.

· Vendor management and contract negotiation support

· RFI/RFP development, response management, and analysis

· Vendor bake-off trial development, execution, analysis

· Project tracking

· Contingency planning

· Problem resolution

· Status reporting

· Supply chain management

· Launch plan evaluation
