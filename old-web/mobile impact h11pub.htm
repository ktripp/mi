Mobile Impact Consulting Practice

The wireless industry is undergoing dramatic change as mobile data grows in importance to carriers and vendors. Open mobile web services, compelling new applications, and smartphones are exponentially increasing the use of mobile data services. Mobile Impact has built its practice around enabling operators and core technology companies to capitalize on these developments.

The explosion of non-traditional wireless devices has also radically changed the landscape of OEM and carrier product portfolios. These emerging devices include telemetry monitors, distributed control systems, smart grid communication systems, e-readers, medical devices, and wirelessly enabled consumer products. Mobile Impact has responded to this trend by building a Smart Grid and Emerging Devices consulting practice supporting the development and deployment of these non-traditional devices by applying our wireless technology expertise to the utility and consumer electronics markets. This practice also develops advanced product certification processes   to enable effective activation and network traffic management for such devices.

Mobile Impact, since its founding in 2007, provides professional and technical consulting services to the wireless market in the areas of mobile data systems and handset/device product management. We view the mobile space as a client-server system and bring combined expertise in both mobile devices and network systems to our projects.

Our core competency is development of product and service requirements combining engineering expertise with sound business cases. As inputs to these requirements we provide strategy development and custom research services. We also support the implementation of these requirements with project/program management, contract development, process development and vendor management services.

Our team of Principal Consultants average over 20 years of experience across all aspects of the industry. Our team is ready to provide high impact, real-world results with a dedication to quality and timeliness.

Our systems approach to new product and service launch combines network, handset, and software to assure project success.
