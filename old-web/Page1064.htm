Value Proposition

Handset Product Management Services

The pace of handset advances combined with the rapid deployment of new data services produces a complex equation of variables for the selection of the right mix of mobile phones in a carrier portfolio.

Mobile Impact provides the research and analysis to select the right mix of handsets to maximize usage and brand identity. Whether developing a new product mix, support for a new service, or a comprehensive evaluation of an existing portfolio, Mobile Impact provides the expertise needed to drive business growth while improving customer satisfaction.

Our service fast-tracks the selection of the right mobile devices for a particular service or target market segment.

· Rapid and deep analysis of available handsets for a service offering or tier

· Custom analysis of feature sets necessary for a particular service

· Tiered strategy development based on target demographics & revenue models

· Life cycle management, product phase-in/phase-out plans

· Vendor management services for handset selection and procurement

· Maximization of user experience impact & consistency across platforms
